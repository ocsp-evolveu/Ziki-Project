Our Services

Are you a painting artist, photographer or musician located in the City of Calgary? Are you looking for ways to increase the awareness of the general public about your work and generate some following? Are you seeking for a new way to monetize your work and unlock different avenues of growth?

If you answered yes to any of the questions above, look no further. Ziki offers you an avenue to grow your brand and be part of an online community while also turning your passion into income.

Online Presence

Do you regularly churn out great art work or content and you would like to make your craft visible to the world? Look no further! We provide you with a platform where you can upload these diamonds and showcase your creative prowress.

Promotion

We not only provide you with a platform to showcase your talent, we also take it upon ourselves to draw attention to your creative work. What's more? We will also engage with industry players to get your foot into the local creative scene.

Sales

We will help you turn your passion into income. As we draw the attention of the public to your creative work, we provide an avenue for them to purchase your work, thereby giving you some extra income. Fans will also have an opportunity to support your work.

Events

As a way of further promoting your creative work, we organise regular events through which creatives like yourself can show case your work. We will also secure opportunities for you to display your talent at local events within the city.

Community

Registering on our platform and uploading your creative work automatically makes you a member of a large online community of like-minded professionals with which you can engage, forge bonds, collaborate and create magic.
